Minecraft is a sandbox video game that lets players explore, build, and survive in a blocky, procedurally generated world. Players can gather resources, craft tools, construct buildings, and face challenges like monsters and hunger. With creative and survival modes, Minecraft encourages creativity, exploration, and problem-solving, making it one of the most popular and versatile games for all ages.
